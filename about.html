ABOUT BUN BUN

Our Story

We are a small bakery that specializes in cinnamon rolls, from our famous original cinnamon rolls to a variety of artisan flavors. Our shop is located in Pittsburgh, PA and we ship fresh rolls anywhere in the Pittsburgh area. Visitors to our site can buy cinnamon rolls by different flavors.
